development projects

Randomeal

Randomeal is a mobile first restaurant selection app for indecisive eaters. It uses a geolocator gem to get the user's current location. The user simply inputs the travel range using the slider, selects the categories to avoid, selects price range and clicks randomize to find an amazing restaurant in his or her neighborhood.

Rioserve

Rioserve is a restaurant reservation app for restaurants in Rio de Janeiro. As a user you can simply login, choose a restaurant and make a reservation. As a restaurant owner, You can create a restaurant and input all your restaurant details.

Mixer

Mixer is a cocktail creation web app. A user can search for his or her favorite cocktail or create a cocktail of their own. The cocktail objects were created by seeding from an api with over 250 cocktails.
